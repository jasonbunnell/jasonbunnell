# Best Bread Dough Recipe 2.0 by Brian Lagerstorm
#### Ingredients
1. 250g warm water 86 degrees
2. 2g instant yeast
3. 300g ripe poolish
4. 12g salt
5. 50g whole wheat
6. 400g bread flour
#### Ripe Poolish - ferment 4 to 24 hours
1. 150g water room temp
2. 1 gram of yeast or less
3. 150g bread flour
#### Directions
1. combine ingredients and mix with stand mixer for 4-5 minutes
2. rest 30 minutes
3. stretch and fold 7 times and form dough ball
4. rest 30 minutes
5. 2nd stretch and fold 7 times and form dough ball with seams underneath on parchment sheet
6. rest 60 minutes
