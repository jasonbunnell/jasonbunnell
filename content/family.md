For more information, you can look at williambunnellfamily.org.  I believe all Bunnell’s in the US are a decedent of William Bunnell.  He was a settler of the Massachusetts Bay Commonwealth in 1630s during the “great migration” and came over in the Winthrop fleet.  You can find out more about the Puritan migration to Massachusetts and their elected governor at [The Winthrop Society](https://www.winthropsociety.com/) site.  And thanks to my Atlanta Refugees I know that William Bunnell likely heard the sermon God’s Promise to His Plantation by John Cotton before embarking for the new world.
